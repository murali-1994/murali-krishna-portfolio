My professional profile

Hello there. I'm Murali Krishna, a product designer by profession and a creative problem-solver by passion. My journey from a mechanical engineering background to UI/UX design has been filled with challenges and achievements, each milestone shaping me into the designer I am today. I believe that failures strengthen my resolve, while successes bring joy to my team and clients alike.

My design career formally began after I prepared for the CEED exam from IIT Bombay in 2019-2020. The experience opened my eyes to the potential of creative design and set me on a path toward my design aspirations. I soon ventured into freelance work as a UI Designer at Thryakshari Software Solutions Pvt. Ltd. in Tirupati from May to November 2021. This role allowed me to explore my creativity and apply design principles to real-world projects, laying the groundwork for my professional growth.

In November 2021, I joined Qentelli Solutions Pvt. Ltd. in Hyderabad, where I shifted focus to Customer Experience (CX). During my time there, which spanned nearly two years, I learned the refinements of customer-centered desing and gained insights into creating seamless user experiences. The experience enriched my understanding of user behavior, helping me become a more empathetic and solution-oriented designer. Alongside, I completed my certification with the Interaction Design Foundation, Further solidifying my design knowledge.

In July 2024, I began my current journey as a Product Designer at RJ Global Solutions in Hyderabad. Here, I am focused on transforming products into intuitive, user-centric experiences, continually pushing the boundaries of design to create solutions that meet both business goals and user needs.

Every step of my journey has been driven by a desire to grow, adapt, and contribute to meaningful projects. I look forward to the exciting possibilities that lie ahead and to making an impact through purposeful design.
